    About

    Zhenyu Guo (郭振宇) is a Lead Researcher in the System Research Group, Microsoft Research Asia.

    Research Interests include distributed systems, operating systems, compilers, and development tools. I’m now devoting most of my time to the open source project at https://github.com/Microsoft/rDSN. The goal is to largely improve the current practice of building distributed systems, in terms of engineering efficiency, quality, as well as end system robustness.

    Projects

    Robust Distributed System Nucleus (rDSN)

    Established: May 15, 2015
